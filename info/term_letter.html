Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Date: 1/14/1976

To Darnell Mantell,

Dear Darnell Mantell,

We hope this letter finds you well. It is with regret that we inform you that your position at MIT is hereby terminated, effective immediately. Despite previous warnings regarding the lack of scientific basis in your research and use of time, you have continued to pursue extraterrestrial phenomena. As a result, your recent work has shown a decline in rigor. We can no longer support your endeavors, as we do not consider them academically sound. We wish you the best in your future pursuits.

We must also insist on a formal cease and desist. Your most recent publications are no longer covered under the academic name of MIT, as they fail to meet our standards. Please retract the MIT affiliation from the following papers:

"Theoretical Pathways from Distant Star Systems and Potential Crash Scenarios of Advanced Civilizations"

"Hypothetical Dimensional Shifting as a Means to Surpass Light-Speed Travel"

"Probability of societal advancement in a post nuclear fission environment"

"Conceptualizing Unmeasurable Forces: A Framework for measuring forces exerted by a soul"

Failure to comply with this request will result in further action.

Sincerely,

Dr. Mary Smith, Dean of Science

Massachusetts Institute of Technology
