Marriott Hotel

Recently opened. Still smells like fresh paint.

Brand new and conspicuously out of place, the Marriott rose up on the edge of town within the last couple of years. The lobby is clean, the staff are professional, and everything works the way it's supposed to.

It draws the kind of guests who have somewhere to be — contractors, government people passing through, the occasional businessman. The ice machine is always stocked. The continental breakfast starts at six sharp.
