Red Roof Inn

Vacancy. Always vacancy.

The kind of place that never quite recovered from something — though no one remembers exactly what. The foundation has shifted enough that none of the doors hang quite right, and the carpets in the walkways have gone stiff with age and weather.

The pool sits behind a chain-link fence, filled with water gone green from algae. A faded sign nearby lists the pool hours. Nobody has bothered to take it down.
