Vic's Garage

Part repair shop, part junkyard, all rust and grit.

Vic’s Garage is basically a junkyard with a dash of repair. Out back sit years of totaled vehicles, rusted metal, and tall weeds swallowing whole fenders. The “shop” is a tin-roof building with a single bay, one car jack, and an oil-stained concrete floor that never quite dries.
