Shadow People

Rumor File — silhouettes in doorways, corners that feel crowded.

People around Dexter describe the same thing with different words: a human shape with no face, standing where it shouldn’t fit.

The sightings spike after long nights, stress, or illness — but that doesn’t explain why multiple witnesses report the same figure in the same places, weeks apart. And it doesn’t explain the footprints that stop in the middle of a hallway.
