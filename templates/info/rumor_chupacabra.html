Chupacabra

Rumor File — livestock deaths, puncture marks, and nobody wanting to talk.

Ranchers around Dexter don’t call the sheriff anymore. They call each other. Goats found drained, fences cut clean, dogs too nervous to go near the pens.

The story changes depending on who tells it — hairless dog, lizard thing, kangaroo hops — but the pattern stays the same: something hungry, something fast, and something that doesn’t leave tracks where it should.
