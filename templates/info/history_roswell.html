The Roswell Incident

History File

In the summer of 1947, something came down outside Roswell, New Mexico. Early reports described the recovery of a “flying disc,” a claim that was quickly walked back by military officials. Within days, the incident was reframed as a mundane weather balloon recovery. Those who have studied the case note the speed of the reversal, the volume of redactions, and the consistency of witness accounts suggesting advanced, unfamiliar materials.
