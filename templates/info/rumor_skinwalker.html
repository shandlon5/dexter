Skinwalker

Rumor File — voices that aren’t right, animals that watch too long.

Locals don’t say the name out loud. They say “that thing” or “don’t go out there.” The rumor usually starts the same way: a familiar voice calling from the dark, using the wrong words in the wrong order.

Dogs whine and refuse to leave the porch. Coyotes stop howling mid-note. If you see something standing upright where it shouldn’t, the advice is simple: don’t stare, don’t follow, and don’t answer if it speaks first.
