Player Characters

The four (five) people who ended up in Dexter for very different reasons.

The Team
FBI

Norris Cornwell

Special Agent — Washington D.C.
Vietnam vet. Former deep cover operative. Sent to Dexter by Director Webster to investigate a person of interest before the CIA can take the case.
Family: Wife Gennifer · Ex-wife Jesse · Kids Phillip & Willie (Philly)
Tell: Reaches for his 8-track when he's on edge
CIA

Franklin Reed

Case Officer — Langley
Vietnam vet. Survivor of the Hanoi Hilton POW camp. Sent by Director Casey to Dexter to beat the FBI to a sensitive case.
Faith: Believes deeply in good and evil
Anchor: A priest he still communicates with regularly
Tell: Hypervigilant in crowds
Academic

Professor Palmer

Anthropologist — New Mexico State University
Specialist in myth, religion, and the recurrence of belief systems across human cultures. Drawn to Dexter by a personal connection to the case.
Expertise: Cross-cultural mythology and religion
Tell: Frames everything through an academic lens
Civilian

Turk

Off the Grid — New Mexico Desert
Lives in a repurposed bus in the desert. Connected to the local Native tribe. Finds herself drawn into the events surrounding Dexter through her work in the region.
Ties: Local Native tribe
Tell: Reads the land. Notices what others miss.
